When it comes to looking at homes...

Something most home buyers tend to focus on when looking to purchase a home is neighborhood safety, it is the best way to see if that HOA fee or mello roos tax is really worth investing in, and the best way to check for a neighborhood's safety is by using CRIME WATCH. In terms of crime there are two types of crime we focus on that center around safety and homeownership and that's crimes against persons, also called personal crimes, which include murder, aggravated assault, rape, and robbery and then there are property crimes involve the theft of property without bodily harm, such as burglary, larceny, auto theft, and arson.
